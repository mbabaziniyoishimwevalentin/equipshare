### What we're building
A rental marketplace where users order equipment, pay, receive invoice PDFs via email, and owners are notified. Now adding a Local Community & Trust module with RSVPs.

### Payments architecture
- **No real payment gateway** — "Pay Now" immediately marks the order as PAID
- Flow: Cart ➔ Place Order (status PENDING) ➔ InitiatePayment (marks PAID, status ➔ PROCESSING) ➔ Redirect to order detail page
- Pay Now button shows on order detail page when `!order.payment || order.payment.status === 'PENDING'`
- Download Invoice button shows when `order.payment.status === 'PAID'`

### RSVP Flow
- Renter RSVPs to a meetup/event → status `PENDING`
- Owner sees pending RSVPs on dashboard → clicks Accept or Reject
- Reject prompts for a reason
- Status update triggers notification to renter
- Renter sees "Accepted ✓", "Rejected: reason", or "RSVP Pending"

### Tax display logic
- New orders: `subtotal`/`tax` stored in DB from cart page
- Old orders (fallback): `subtotal = sum of item totalAmounts`, `tax = subtotal × 0.18`
- Invoice PDF: subtotal/tax/Total Paid (incl. tax), "Generated by: Owner Name(s)"

### Currency
- Rwf throughout (no $)
- `.toLocaleString()` for formatting

### Open issues
